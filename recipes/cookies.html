Lliam's Classic Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

These are delicious cookies made by Lliam Rose, they have the perfect ratio of gooeyness too softness and the whole family loves them!

Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 2/3 cup light brown sugar, packed 200g
  • 2/3 cup sugar 200g
  • 12 tablespoons butter 500g
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla
  • 1 teaspoon vinegar, 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 large egg
  • 2 cups flour 500
  • 2 cups chocolate chips 500

Steps

  1. Preheat the Oven to 375 degrees Farenheit
  2. Grab a pot to cook butter until brown, then wait till cool
  3. Mix all wet ingredients into a bowl then once butter is cool add browned butter.
  4. Mix all dry ingredients in a seperate bowl
  5. Put the wet ingredients bowl into the dry and mix
  6. Grab a metal baking sheet and an ice cream scoop and spray with oil
  7. With ice cream scoop, scoop the cookie dough onto the baking sheet
  8. Cook for 12 - 13 minutes then enjoy!
